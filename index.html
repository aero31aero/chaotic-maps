Implementation of Chaos Based Cryptosystems

Garg Siddharth Janak : 2015B5A80684H

Rohitashva Vashishtha : 2015B4PS0546H

About

Digital chaos-based cryptosystems (also called digital chaotic ciphers) are designed for digital computers, where one or more chaotic maps are implemented in finite computing precision to encrypt the plain-message in a number of ways, such as the following ones:

1. Stream ciphers based on chaos-based PRNG (pseudo-random number generators).

2. Chaotic stream ciphers with ciphertext feedback mode.

3. Block ciphers based on chaotic round function or S-boxes.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We hypothesize that the following should hold true:

1. The chaotic ciphertexts hold up to the standard cryptographic attacks.

2. The chaotic S-boxes compare to DES and AES S-boxes selection criteria.

3. The chaotic key generation schemes are determinably better than PRNG based schemes.
